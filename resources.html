Articles to Read from Internet

Water Pollution: Everything You Need to Know This comprehensive article from the National Geographic Society provides an overview of water pollution, its causes, effects, and solutions. It covers topics such as the different types of water pollution, the impact on human health and the environment, and what individuals can do to make a difference. https://www.nationalgeographic.com/environment/article/freshwater-pollution

The World's Water Pollution Crisis: This article from the World Resources Institute delves into the global water pollution crisis, exploring the extent of the problem and its impact on people and ecosystems around the world. It also highlights the need for urgent action to address this critical issue. https://www.wri.org/water

Water Pollution Facts and Statistics: This informative page from the Environmental Protection Agency (EPA) provides a collection of facts and statistics about water pollution in the United States. It covers topics such as the sources of water pollution, the chemicals and pollutants found in polluted water, and the health risks associated with exposure to contaminated water. https://www.epa.gov/water-research/water-research-fact-sheets

Water Pollution: Causes, Effects, and Solutions:This educational article from the United States Geological Survey (USGS) offers a comprehensive explanation of water pollution, its causes, and potential solutions. It provides detailed information on the different types of water pollution, the impact on human health and the environment, and strategies for preventing and controlling water pollution. https://www.nrdc.org/stories/water-pollution-everything-you-need-know

Water Pollution Prevention and Control: This informative page from the United Nations Environment Programme (UNEP) focuses on water pollution prevention and control measures. It outlines various approaches to preventing water pollution, including wastewater treatment, industrial pollution control, and agricultural best practices. https://www.unep.org/explore-topics/water

Clean Water for All: This website from the United Nations (UN) provides a global perspective on water pollution and the importance of clean water for all. It highlights the UN's Sustainable Development Goals related to water and sanitation and showcases initiatives and partnerships aimed at achieving clean water for all. https://www.un.org/sustainabledevelopment/water-and-sanitation/

Water Pollution Resources for Kids and Teens: This page from the National Oceanic and Atmospheric Administration (NOAA) offers a collection of educational resources for kids and teens about water pollution. It includes interactive games, videos, and activities that help children understand the importance of clean water and the ways they can prevent water pollution. https://oceanservice.noaa.gov/education/tutorial_pollution/welcome.html

Water Pollution Action Steps: This website from the Water Environment Federation (WEF) provides a guide to individual actions that can help prevent water pollution. It offers simple tips and suggestions for everyday activities, such as conserving water, properly disposing of household chemicals, and maintaining septic systems. https://www.wef.org/topics/practice-areas/water-and-wastewater-treatment/

Water Pollution: Get InvolvedThis page from the EPA encourages individuals to get involved in water pollution prevention and protection efforts. It provides information on volunteer opportunities, advocacy groups, and educational resources to help individuals make a difference in their communities. https://www.epa.gov/enforcement/water-enforcement

Clean Water America Alliance: This non-profit organization works to protect and restore water resources in the United States. Their website provides a wealth of information about water pollution, including educational resources, advocacy tools, and news updates on water quality issues. https://uswateralliance.org/
